Introduction

whose goal is to clean as much man-made debris
from our waters. Our efforts span all over the World
in over 100 countries, in all 7 continents! We get
on-sight help from volunteers willing to travel to
the site and local volunteers. We use the money to
pay for flights, equipment, and food for our
kind volunteers
